# Literary Quotes — Inspiration Library

**Purpose**: A collection of quotes that might resonate. Not a search system, just inspiration.

**How to use**: Read through when you need something. If a quote fits, use it. If nothing fits, don't force it.

---

## Rilke (里尔克)

### On Endurance
**CN**: "有何胜利可言，挺住意味着一切。"
**EN**: "What matters is to win through, to endure."
**Source**: Letters to a Young Poet

### On Solitude
**CN**: "也许所有的龙都是公主，她们只是在等待看到我们勇敢一次。"
**EN**: "Perhaps all the dragons in our lives are princesses who are only waiting to see us act, just once, with beauty and courage."
**Source**: Letters to a Young Poet

### On Growth
**CN**: "要有耐心，对心中未解决的问题都抱有耐心，试着去爱这些问题本身。"
**EN**: "Be patient toward all that is unsolved in your heart and try to love the questions themselves."
**Source**: Letters to a Young Poet

### On Being Yourself
**CN**: "没有什么比看到一个人按照他自己的样子生活更能打动我们的了。"
**EN**: "The only journey is the one within."
**Source**: Letters to a Young Poet

## Eileen Chang / 张爱玲

### On Time
**CN**: "于千万人之中遇见你所要遇见的人，于千万年之中，时间的无涯的荒野里，没有早一步，也没有晚一步，刚巧赶上了。"
**EN**: "Among thousands of people, you meet the one you're meant to meet. Across thousands of years, in the boundless wilderness of time, not a step too early, not a step too late—just in time."
**Source**: Love

### On Life's Messiness
**CN**: "生命是一袭华美的袍，爬满了蚤子。"
**EN**: "Life is a beautiful robe, full of fleas."
**Source**: A Dream of Genius

### On Loneliness
**CN**: "笑，全世界便与你同声笑；哭，你便独自哭。"
**EN**: "Laugh, and the world laughs with you. Cry, and you cry alone."
**Source**: Withered Flowers

### On Small Things
**CN**: "人生最可爱的地方就在这些琐碎的小事上。"
**EN**: "The loveliest parts of life are in these small, trivial things."
**Source**: Written on Water

### On Understanding
**CN**: "因为懂得，所以慈悲。"
**EN**: "Because of understanding, there is compassion."
**Source**: Love in a Fallen City

## Lao Tzu / 老子

### On Non-Striving
**CN**: "上善若水。水善利万物而不争。"
**EN**: "The highest good is like water. Water benefits all things without striving."
**Source**: Tao Te Ching, Chapter 8

### On Doing Without Doing
**CN**: "无为而无不为。"
**EN**: "By doing nothing, everything is done."
**Source**: Tao Te Ching, Chapter 48

### On Softness
**CN**: "天下莫柔弱于水，而攻坚强者莫之能胜。"
**EN**: "Nothing in the world is softer than water, yet nothing is better at overcoming the hard and strong."
**Source**: Tao Te Ching, Chapter 78

### On Emptiness
**CN**: "埏埴以为器，当其无，有器之用。"
**EN**: "We shape clay into a pot, but it is the emptiness inside that makes it useful."
**Source**: Tao Te Ching, Chapter 11

### On Journey
**CN**: "千里之行，始于足下。"
**EN**: "A journey of a thousand miles begins with a single step."
**Source**: Tao Te Ching, Chapter 64

### On Not-Knowing
**CN**: "知不知，上；不知知，病。"
**EN**: "To know that you do not know is highest. To not know yet think you know is a disease."
**Source**: Tao Te Ching, Chapter 71

## How to Use These

**Don't**:
- Search by theme/temperature/emotion
- Force quotes into every response
- Use quotes as explanations

**Do**:
- Read through naturally
- Use when something genuinely fits
- Let quotes breathe—don't over-explain them
- Skip quoting entirely if nothing resonates

**Remember**: A response without any quote can be just as good. Sometimes better.
